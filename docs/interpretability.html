Model Interpretability
In our EPSRC-funded project, “Twenty20Insights”, we aim to investigate explainable AI (XAI) approaches which can provide interpretations both faithful to model decisions and are also better understood by humans. Most existing XAI approaches aim at identifying input features such as words or phrases important for model predictions. Neural models developed in NLP however often compose word semantics in a hierarchical manner. Interpretation by words or phrases only thus cannot faithfully explain model decisions. We have proposed a Hierarchical Interpretable Neural Text classifier, called Hint, which is able to identify the latent semantic factors and their compositions which contribute to the model’s final decisions. This is often beyond what word-level interpretations could capture.
